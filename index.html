WHO ARE WE?

Established in 2015, FIRST Robotics Competition Team #4617, otherwise called DAUN, is an all-girls community robotics team based at Western University in London, Ontario.


DAUN helps sharpen the minds of high-school students by fostering skills in robotics, technology, problem-solving, and critical-thinking. Members are given the chance to gain hands-on experience with robotics, including the planning, designing, mechanics, and programming of a robot. Strong lasting friendships can be made with other members on the team, as well as connections with other mentors and students. Not only does DAUN provides experience in robotics, but there is also experience with business and art!
